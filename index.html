About Us

With a collective experience of over 25 years in HVAC and refrigeration, our expert team at Manik Refrigeration Sales & Service ensures precision, reliability, and excellence in every project.

Recognized for its commitment to quality and reliability, Manik Refrigeration Sales & Service is a leading name in the refrigeration and air-conditioning sector. We operate across India with leading companies, handling projects in Cold Chain, Refrigeration, Fresh & Vegetable Chillers (F&V), and VRF/VRV systems.

We specialize in supplying, installing, commissioning, and maintaining a wide range of systems, including Cold Rooms, VRV/VRF systems, Refrigeration Units, and Air Conditioning equipment for major retail chains and businesses.

To ensure smooth and trouble-free operation throughout the year, we also offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC).

In addition, we offer consultancy services for diverse refrigeration solutions such as open-type chillers, cold rooms, deep freezers, and VRF setups.

Our focus remains on delivering prompt, reliable, and high-quality service, fostering long-term and strong business relationships with our clients.
